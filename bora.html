Boracay Island

Here's a guide for your next trip to Boracay

Brb booking my plane tickets already!

Boracay will continue to be a treasure the Philippines is lucky to have as its own, loved by both locals and tourists from all over the globe. With the world open once again, we’re sure our own kababayans abroad are missing being back at home, and for sure a trip to the Philippines is a great chance to see these white beaches again.

Island Hopping

Island hopping in Boracay isn't just a tour, it's a quintessential Boracay experience. Imagine this: cruising on a traditional paraw sailboat, the wind whipping through your hair as you glide across crystal-clear turquoise waters. The Philippine sun warms your skin, and the only sound is the gentle lapping of waves against the hull. This is the start of your Boracay island hopping adventure. As you sail to different islands, be prepared to be dazzled by the natural beauty of the Philippines. Puka Beach will welcome you with its powdery white sand that shimmers like sugar under the sun. Diniwid Beach, with its unique rock formations jutting out from the shore, offers a dramatic and picturesque scene. These are just a few of the hidden gems you might encounter on your island hopping adventure. But the beauty goes beyond the beaches. The crystal-clear waters surrounding Boracay are teeming with marine life. Strap on your snorkeling gear and dive into a world of vibrant coral reefs and colorful fish. Be amazed by the underwater wonders that await you, a kaleidoscope of life just beneath the surface.

Parasailing

Boracay's iconic white sand beaches transform into ribbons of pristine white against the turquoise canvas of the ocean. Secluded coves and hidden lagoons reveal themselves, sparkling like jewels. The lush greenery of the island blends seamlessly with the vibrant blues of the sky and sea, creating a picture-perfect postcard come to life. But the beauty goes beyond the visuals. Parasailing offers a thrilling sense of weightlessness. Feel the wind whipping through your hair as you soar above the waves. Let the worries of the world melt away as you surrender to the exhilarating freedom of flight. Safety is paramount, of course. Professional operators ensure a smooth and safe experience. Life jackets are provided, and experienced crew members handle the entire process. So, you can simply relax, take in the stunning views, and capture memories that will last a lifetime.

Banana Boat Riding

here's the thing about banana boat rides: they're not meant to be a smooth cruise. Part of the fun is the element of surprise! A well-timed turn by the driver can send you careening towards the refreshing water with a shriek of delight (or maybe a little fear!). Don't worry, though – life vests are mandatory, and experienced professionals are there to ensure everyone's safety. In fact, falling off and getting splashed is often half the fun! Just imagine the exhilaration of popping back up to the surface, the laughter echoing around you, and the feeling of pure joy that comes with a little friendly competition to see who stays on the longest. So, if you're looking for an activity that guarantees laughter, screams (the good kind!), and unforgettable memories, look no further than the banana boat ride. It's the perfect way to bond with your loved ones, create hilarious stories to share back home, and experience the thrill of the waves in a safe and controlled environment. Just remember to hang on tight (or maybe not!), and get ready for a splashingly good time in Boracay!

Crystal Kayak

Forget the #FOMO (fear of missing out)! Boracay offers an experience that practically begs to be documented on social media – the crystal clear kayak photo-op. This unique activity is more than just a pretty picture; it's a chance to create lasting memories and capture the essence of your Boracay adventure. Imagine this: you're nestled comfortably in a transparent kayak, a marvel of modern design. The crystal-clear material creates the illusion that you're practically floating on air, the turquoise waters of Boracay swirling beneath your feet. As you paddle alongside your friendly local guide, the breathtaking scenery unfolds around you. The white sand beach shimmers under the sun, palm trees sway gently in the breeze, and the vast expanse of the ocean stretches out towards the horizon. The beauty of this activity lies in its simplicity. It's perfect for people of all ages and abilities. There's no need for complex instructions or strenuous activity. Just relax, soak in the beauty of Boracay, and let your guide work their magic.

Sunset Paraw sailing

As the curtain closes on another action-packed day in Boracay, there's no more fitting finale than witnessing the island's legendary sunset. Sure, you could admire it from the shore, but why settle for the ordinary when you can experience it in extraordinary fashion? A paraw boat cruise at sunset offers an immersive and unforgettable experience that will leave you breathless. Settle in with your loved ones, whether it's your balikbayan titas and titos (aunts and uncles) or your closest barkada (group of friends). Let the gentle rhythm of the waves lull you into a state of relaxation, and share stories and laughter as you witness this magnificent spectacle together. This isn't just about the view, though. The paraw boat itself adds a touch of authenticity to the experience. These traditional vessels, used by local fishermen for centuries, are a window into Boracay's rich culture and heritage. As you sail along, you become part of the island's story, creating memories that will forever be intertwined with the beauty of the Boracay sunset.
