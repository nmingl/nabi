Klook Philippines

Klook is a one-stop shop for planning the fun parts of your trip, letting you customize your itinerary and potentially find better deals than booking directly with each venue or tour operator.

Tripadvisor Philippines

Tripadvisor is a great resource for finding and researching tours and activities in the Philippines. You can read reviews from other travelers, compare prices, and find activities that interest you. Once you've found a tour or activity that you're interested in, you can usually book it directly through the operator's website.

Traveloka Philippines

This Southeast Asian travel booking platform offers a wide variety of services in the Philippines, including flights, hotels, and activities. They have a good selection of tours, attraction tickets, and other experiences specifically catered to the Philippines.

Kkday Philippines

A major competitor to Klook in Asia, KKday offers similar services focused on tours, activities, and attraction tickets across Asia, with a strong presence in the Philippines. They also provide transportation options and experiences for many popular destinations in the Philippines.

GetGo Philippines

Owned by Cebu Pacific Air, GetGo is a rewards program that also allows you to book flights, hotels, and activities in the Philippines using your GetGo points. They offer a decent selection of tours and activities for popular destinations in the Philippines.
